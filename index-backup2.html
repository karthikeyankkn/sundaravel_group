WHY US

Sundaravel group has exercised complete vertical integration of Match industry. The entire value chain of match making is managed in-house, right from procurement of raw materials to timely delivery of finished goods.

We have established a strong and deep distribution network into the FMCG market of India including rural areas.

Our match stick has a special chemical formulation which ensures reliable flame performance irrespective of extreme weather conditions.

Fully automatic production technology ensures top quality and consistency.

German technology offset printing machines ensure precision accuracy of your design on match labels.
