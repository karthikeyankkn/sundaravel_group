Vision

To delight our Customers, stakeholders & employees by manufacturing high-quality matches at competitive prices and provide value for money experience at every touch point. We attain this by adhering to industry best practices in quality and safety standards.

Mission

The vision of Sundaravel Group is to be a premier manufacturer of quality matches that continue to enlighten billions of homes and lives across the globe.

Vertical Integration

We have exercised complete vertical integration of Match industry. The entire value chain of match making is managed in-house, right from procurement of raw materials to timely delivery of finished goods.

Strong Distribution Network
