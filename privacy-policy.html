Privacy Policy


1. What do we do with your information?

We collect your name, company name, email, and phone number through the contact form on our website. This data will be used to reach out to you to address your queries or business requirements. We will be using this data to call/ email/ SMS to you. We will be using third-party campaign tools for calls / SMS.

2. How do we collect data?

We collect data through the contact form on our website. This is filled by the user based on his / her interest in our products or services. We have Google Analytics installed to understand the website traffic and performance. We will get data through Google Analytics, as well. We do not track our users using cookies or any other internet log mechanisms.

3. How will we use your data?

We collect your data so that our sales executive or customer support team could reach out to you to address your query. We will also be sending emails with details about our products.

4. How do we store your data?

Information submitted over the forms is stored securely in a server in Tamil Nadu, India. The data is well protected as the systems are secured.

5. What are your data protection rights?

You have the rights to request for deletion of your records from our companies database. You may also request us to modify our Privacy Policy as part of your “Right to rectification.” You also have the rights to object processing of your contact information.

All the above can be done by sending an email to enquiry@sundaravelgroup.com

6. How to contact us?

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, send us an email or contact us at: enquiry@sundaravelgroup.com

7. How do I escalate in case of lack of response?

If you wish to report a complaint or if you think that we have not responded to your request in a satisfactory manner, you may send an email to our ceo@sundaravelgroup.com
